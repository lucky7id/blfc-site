About

You have enough to worry about for BLFC, let us take care of getting you and your fuzzy friends to Reno and back! Mike and I have put together a chartered bus that will take us to and from BLFC. For less than the cost of a tank of gas, you’ll earn a spot on this musical journey. Bring some drinks, bring your friends, sit back and before you know it,we’ll be pulling up at the Grand Sierra. All you need to worry about is having as much fun as possible! When the con is over we'll keep the party rolling for a few more hours, as we make our way back to Milbrae!

This is a 21+ bus, and we will need to check Id’s! The name on your Id MUST match the name you sign up with! If there is enough interest from younger furballs, we will look into chartering an additional bus. Trip includes ride to Reno as well as return ride back to Milbrae.

Skip the BLFC Registration line!

As if this weren't cool enough, the awesome furs on the BLFC staff have given us the best perk possible! If you register for the both the bus and BLFC before April 20th, you'll get your con badge and swag right when you board! No waiting in long lines, just show up, settle in, and get your BLFC started right.

A word about luggage

To make sure that space is maximized for fursuit cases, we ask that you try to use the smallest luggage possible. We have reserved the last few rows of the bus for overflow space, and there is overhead storage in addition to the undercarriage luggage space. We are asking for an optional $10 donation for any oversized bags, to help cover the cost of the reserved luggage seats. We thank you in advance for packing as lightly as possible!
