About

You have enough to worry about for BLFC, let us take care of getting you and your fuzzy friends to Reno! Mike and I have put together a chartered bus that will take us to and from BLFC. For less than the cost of a tank of gas, you’ll earn a spot on this musical journey. Bring some drinks, bring your friends, sit back and before you know it,we’ll be pulling up at the Grand Sierra. All you need to worry about is having as much fun as possible!

This is a 21+ bus, and we will need to check ID’s! If there is enough interest from younger furballs, we will look into chartering an additional bus.
