About
You have enough to worry about for BLFC, let us take care of getting you and your fuzzy friends to Reno and back! We have put together a chartered bus that will take us to and from BLFC. For less than the cost of a tank of gas, you’ll earn a spot in the party as we embark on a great adventure. Bring some drinks, bring your friends, sit back and before you know it, we’ll be pulling up at the Grand Sierra. All you need to worry about is having as much fun as possible! When the con is over we'll keep the party rolling for a few more hours, as we make our way back to Milbrae!
This is a 21+ bus, and we will need to check Id’s! The name on your Id MUST match the name you sign up with! Trip includes ride to Reno as well as return ride back to Milbrae.
Skip the BLFC Reg line
We all hate line con, so take the bus and skip it! BLFC has been kind enough to give us a special priorty registration for anyone that rides the bus. This means that when we arrive at the GSR, staff will be ready to hand you your registration as soon as you step off the bus.
Luggage
The bus can hold a large amount of bags, but the space is limited per passenger. A standard ticket will include space for 1 bag. You will be able to select more baggage space, but an additional cost will be applied
Additional info
  • This is a round trip, price includes pickup and return from Milbrae
  • 21+ Must bring valid ID that matches name on reservation
  • 50 seats available
  • Alcohol is permitted, No glass containers
  • Suits are okay and encouraged, but there will not be a dedicated changing tent
  • Entertainment will be a collaborative spotify playlist
  • Tips will go towards providing water and snacks on the bus, and the time put in
